Lavendar Test Page

This page is meant for controlled extension testing. It includes true flashing surfaces, generated video clips with different motion intensity, calmer motion that should stay visible, and Giphy embeds so you can check the extension against a real-world source.

Keep screen brightness low while testing, and close the tab if anything feels uncomfortable.

  • The fast flashing block, blinking feed card, color-flash video, flashing Giphy GIF, and flashing Giphy embed should blur.
  • The fast-motion demo video should blur, while the slow-motion demo video should stay visible.
  • The pulsing panel, static image, and calmer Giphy GIF and embed should stay visible.
  • The inserted flashing feed item should get picked up shortly after it appears.
